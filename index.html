● Spin + Power

Pros: lots of spin, good power and more comfort. Cons: loses tension faster and needs more frequent restrings.

● Spin + Control

Pros: high spin with control and predictability; holds tension. Cons: harder on the arm, less power, demands good technique.

● Comfort + Power

Pros: soft, comfortable and powerful, ideal if you feel it in the arm. Cons: less spin and control; loses tension sooner.

● Control + Durability

Pros: precise control, stable tension and lasts longer between restrings. Cons: little spin, stiffer feel and less power.

Polyester strings only (the spin/control family) — 200 models, 1 representative gauge (≈ 1.25 mm) per model. "Tour (ATP)" and "Club favourites" are curated lists (tour usage + BR/UK retail presence); the dataset carries no popularity metric. Natural gut and multifilament are not in this data. Lab measurements (Tennis Warehouse University), fast swing. The dashed lines mark the median stiffness and spin.
